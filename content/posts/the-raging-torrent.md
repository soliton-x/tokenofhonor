I'm often reminded of this quote from Bertolt Brecht.

DE: "Über die Gewalt Der reißende Strom wird gewalttätig genannt Aber das Flußbett, das ihn einengt Nennt keiner gewalttätig."

FR: "On parle toujours de la violence du fleuve,<br>
jamais de celle des berges qui l'enserrent."

EN: "The raging torrent is called violent,<br>
But the river-bed that constrains it,<br>
None calls it violent.”

If I wanted to blame others, society, it wouldn't feel right.
If I wanted to blame my own mind, it wouldn't really help.

It has been raging since my youngest age, and it is still raging. 

What is the river-bed constraining my torrent?
